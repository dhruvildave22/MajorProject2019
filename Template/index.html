Shri Prakash Javdekar

Hon'ble Minister of HRD

Prakash Javadekar is an Indian politician. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and is the incumbent Union Minister of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India.

Shri Upendra Kushwaha

Hon'ble Minister of State for HRD

Upendra Kushwaha is an Indian politician and Minister of State for HRD in Government of India. He is a Member of Parliament From Karakat loksabha in rohtas district, a kushwaha caste dominated consitiuency in Bihar.
